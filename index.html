    About Me

    Experience

    4+ years
    Teacher, Computer Science and Engineering

    Education

    BSc in Computer Science and Engineering,
    Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Medical Imaging and Applications

    I am currently employed as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh. I pursued my Master’s degree in 2023, specializing in Medical Image Analysis, through an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree Program coordinated by the University of Girona, with funding provided by the European Union. I did my MSc thesis, titled “Temporal Image Registration using deep learning for 3D Fetal Echocardiography,” under the supervision of Dr. Choon Hwai Yap at Imperial College London.

    Throughout my master's, I got the opportunity to undertake a summer internship at the research laboratory of Institut Pascal in France. During this internship, I focused on 'Diagnostic and therapeutic management of superficial tumors of the digestive tract assisted by artificial intelligence,' under the guidance of Dr. Adrien Bartoli. Additionally, I actively participated in the 16th EXCITE Zurich Summer School on Biomedical Imaging with the participation grant, hosted by the University and ETH Zurich.

    I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology in 2019, achieving first class first honors. My graduation thesis, titled “Efficient Partitioning Algorithm for Parallel Multidimensional Matrix Operations by Linearization,” was supervised by Dr. K. M. Azharul Hasan.

    My current research interests encompass Medical Image Analysis and Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on Deep Learning and its applications in the field of medicine and diagnosis. Find my complete CV here...

    Projects

    • 3D Fetal Echocardiography Image segmentation Dataset Annotation

      Part of my master's thesis in Imperial College London (2023)

      The 4D fetal echo images were collected and properly annotated with the help of an existing cardiac motion estimation algorithm.

    • Retinal blood vessel segmentation in fundus images

      Part of my image Processing course project in Master's (2022)

      A procedure to segment thin and thick retinal vessels to help medical analysis and diagnosis of related diseases.

    • Temporal Image Registration using deep learning for 3D Fetal Echocardiography

      Part of my master's thesis in Imperial College London (2023)

      We have proposed to develop methods for automatic 3D segmentation based on temporal registration from 4D fetal echo images.

    • Colorectal Lesions Segmentation assisted by Artificial Intelligence

      Part of my summer internship project with Pascal Institue, France (2022)

      We have applied deep learning models to segment Green-colored areas in laterally spreading tumors on narrow-band imaging for Colorectal Lesions.

    • Skin Lesions Segmentation and Classification

      Part of my project in 2nd and 3rd Semester of Master's (2022-2023)

      We have applied various traditional Image Processing, Machine Learning and Deep Learning models to segment and classify Skin Lesions.

    • Brain MRI Tissue Segmentation

      Part of my project in the 3rd Semester of Master's (2023)

      We have applied traditional and Deep Learning models to segment Brain Tissue Classes and Compared Performances.

    • Chest CT Volumes Registration

      Part of my project in the 3rd Semester of Master's (2023)

      We have applied traditional and Deep Learning models to register Chest CT Volumes and Compared Performances.
